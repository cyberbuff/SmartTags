Second youngest grand master ever, from Chennai
Here is a heart-warming story.  Coming from a very modest background, this boy Praggnanandhaa Rameshbabu has become the world's second youngest grand master ever, at the age of 12 years, 10 months.
https://www.thenewsminute.com/article/making-champion-how-praggnanandhaa-became-india-s-youngest-chess-grandmaster-83597
On 23 June 2018 he achieved his third and final GM norm at the Gredine Open in Urtijëi by defeating GM Luca Moroni in the eighth round to become, at the age of 12 years, 10 months and 13 days, the second youngest person ever to achieve the status of grandmaster (Sergey Karjakin achieved the title at the age of 12 years and 7 months).[7]
